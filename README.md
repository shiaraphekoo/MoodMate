MoodMate
Overview
MoodMate is a user-friendly mobile application designed to help you track your daily emotions and mental well-being. By logging your mood and adding notes, you can gain valuable insights into your emotional trends over time. The app provides a clean and intuitive interface, making it easy to record your feelings and visualize your emotional journey through an interactive calendar and mood trend graphs.

Features
User Authentication: Secure login and sign-up pages to ensure your data is kept private.

Daily Mood Logging: Easily log your current mood by selecting one of five emoji options: angry, sad, neutral, happy, and excited.

Journaling: Add notes to each mood entry to provide context and detail about your day.

Data Visualization:

Mood Calendar: View your moods for the month at a glance with a color-coded calendar.

Mood Trends: See a graphical representation of your mood trends over the past month.

Customization: Switch between light and dark modes to personalize the app's theme.

Data Storage: Mood entries and user information are securely stored in a Firebase database.

Design and Technical Considerations
Purpose
The primary purpose of MoodMate is to provide a simple and effective tool for self-awareness and mental wellness. In today's fast-paced world, it's easy to overlook our emotional health. This app addresses that by providing a private space for users to track their feelings, helping them identify patterns and triggers that affect their mood. This process encourages self-reflection and personal growth.

Tech Stack
Frontend: xml

Backend & Database: Firebase, Android Studio

Platform: Android

UI/UX Design
The app's design prioritizes a minimalist and clean aesthetic to ensure a calming user experience. The color palette, primarily featuring calming shades of blue, purple, and white, is chosen to be gentle on the eyes. The interface is straightforward, with clear buttons and intuitive navigation that allows users to seamlessly log their mood, view their history, and adjust settings.
